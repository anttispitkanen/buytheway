The cheapest way to get your groceries delivered to you

Crowdsourced grocery delivery

Here's how it works:

Want to order groceries?

1. Choose the groceries you want to get from our easy-to-use mobile application.

2. Pay the groceries with your credit card. The delivery fee is only 5-10€. It's cheaper than any existing home delivery services.

3. Your groceries are delivered to you by someone who's going to the store anyway. You save valuable time and trouble.

Want to deliver groceries while doing your own grocery shopping?

1. Go do you own grocery shopping in a supermarket.

2. Open our easy-to-use app to see if there are any orders by people who live near you or on your way home.

3. If there are orders, pick the ordered groceries up. We pay you directly to your bank account through our app.

4. You get a pick up fee of 2-5€ - you basically get your own groceries cheaper!

5. Take the groceries to the orderer on your way home - no extra effort required.
